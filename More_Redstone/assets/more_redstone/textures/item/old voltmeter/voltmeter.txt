Redstone Voltmeter


Wire

-place item into item frame.

-place redstone in front, above, or below item frame.

-to show redstone voltage or level press shift or sneak.


Wireless

-hold voltage meter in main hand

-right click onto redstone wire to get voltage.


Diamond voltage meter

-same as regular voltage meter but you can also detect other redstone:

-dropper/dispenser is on/off

-lever is on/off

-hopper is locked

-chest is locked
